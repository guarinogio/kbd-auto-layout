# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be welcoming, respectful, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- being respectful and professional
- giving constructive feedback
- assuming good intent
- focusing on technical issues instead of personal attacks
- being open to review and discussion

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or discrimination
- personal insults or abusive language
- trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- publishing private information without permission
- hostile or bad-faith participation

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, issues, or other interactions that do not follow this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer directly.
